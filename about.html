About

Welcome to 360 KITO

Strategically positioned along the bustling Nakuru-Nairobi Highway, 360 KITO is Nakuru's premier business centre designed to serve the dynamic needs of both travelers and the local community. Our prime location offers unparalleled accessibility, making us the ideal stopover destination for those journeying between Kenya's major cities.

We recognized the tremendous opportunity in this thriving corridor and created a modern, comprehensive business hub that caters to diverse commercial needs. From retail and dining to professional services and entertainment, 360 KITO brings together everything you need under one roof.

Prime Location, Endless Opportunity

Our strategic position on one of Kenya's busiest highways ensures consistent foot traffic and visibility. Whether you're a traveler seeking convenience or a local business looking for the perfect location, 360 KITO offers the exposure and accessibility that drives success.

The Nakuru-Nairobi corridor represents one of East Africa's most significant commercial routes, and we've positioned ourselves at its heart. This isn't just a business centre—it's a gateway to opportunity, connecting businesses with thousands of potential customers daily.

Built for Modern Business

360 KITO features state-of-the-art facilities designed to accommodate a wide range of businesses. Our modern infrastructure, ample parking, and professional environment create the perfect foundation for your business to thrive. Join a vibrant community of forward-thinking entrepreneurs and established brands in Nakuru's most exciting commercial development.
